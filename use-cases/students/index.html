Students & Academics

Compile, organize, and compress homework or research files locally.

Real-World Scenarios

Lab Reports & Diagram Merging

A science student scans hand-drawn charts and diagram sheets, then merges them with a written report document into a single, cohesive PDF submission.

Textbook Chapter Extracting

A student splits a massive 300MB scanned PDF textbook to extract only the 2 specific chapters required for a group project presentation.

LMS Upload Optimization

An engineering student compresses a detailed report loaded with high-resolution graphs to bypass the 10MB upload limit on the student portal.

Lecture Slide Conversion

A student converts lecture slides to editable Word files to extract textbook definitions and compile structured study notes.

Why Local Processing Matters

Thesis drafts, research papers, and assignments represent your personal intellectual property. Client-side processing ensures your drafts are never harvested by AI scrapers, stored on remote clouds, or exposed to unauthorized databases.
