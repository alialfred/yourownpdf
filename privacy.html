Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

🔒 The Most Important Thing:

YOUROWNPDF.COM processes ALL files 100% in your browser. Your files NEVER leave your device. We have no servers that store, process, or even see your files. This isn't just a promise — it's how our technology works.

1. Information We Collect

YOUROWNPDF.COM is designed to collect as little information as possible:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Files: Your PDF, image, and other files never leave your device
  • File Contents: We never see, access, or store any file content
  • Personal Data: We don't require accounts or personal information
  • Payment Info: All tools are free, so no payment data is ever collected

Information We MAY Collect:

  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about which tools are used (no file contents)
  • Browser Info: Basic technical data like browser type for compatibility
  • Local Storage: Theme preferences and first-time-use flags stored only on your device

2. How We Use Information

Any limited data we collect is used only to:

  • Improve tool performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and compatibility issues
  • Understand which tools are most useful to our users
  • Display relevant advertisements (if applicable)

We NEVER sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

YOUROWNPDF.COM uses minimal browser storage:

  • LocalStorage: Stores your theme preference (dark/light mode) and model download status
  • IndexedDB/Cache API: May store AI models for offline use (background remover tool)
  • Cookies: May be used for advertising and analytics

You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • CDN Services: To deliver JavaScript libraries (unpkg, cdnjs)
  • Advertising: Google AdSense or similar (future)
  • Analytics: Google Analytics or similar (anonymous only)

These services have their own privacy policies. We ensure they receive no file data.

5. Data Security

Since your files never leave your device:

  • There is zero risk of server-side data breaches
  • No man-in-the-middle attacks can access your files
  • Your data is as secure as your own device

We recommend keeping your browser and device updated for maximum security.

6. Children's Privacy

YOUROWNPDF.COM does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. All tools are safe for all ages as no data is collected.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of YOUROWNPDF.COM after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@yourownpdf.com or visit our Contact Page.

🔒 YOUROWNPDF.COM — Your files are yours. Always.
