Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to everything we do

🔒 Your Files Never Leave Your Device

Unlike other online tools, Yourpdfown.com processes all files locally in your browser. No uploads, no server storage, no data transmission. Your documents never leave your computer or device.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information. You can use all our tools without creating an account, providing an email, or sharing any identifying details.

Automatically Collected Information: We do not use analytics, tracking cookies, or any form of data collection on our website.

2. Local Storage Usage

We use local storage for a single purpose:

  • Theme Preference: We store your preferred color theme (light or dark) in your browser's localStorage to provide a consistent experience across visits. This is the only persistent data we store.

All processing of your PDF and image files happens in browser memory. Once you close the tab or refresh the page, all data is cleared.

3. How We Process Your Files

All file processing on Yourpdfown.com happens entirely on your device:

  • When you select a file, it's loaded directly into your browser's memory
  • Our JavaScript code processes the file using browser APIs
  • The processed file is made available for you to download
  • No data is ever transmitted to an external server
  • The file data exists only in your browser's temporary memory

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services for processing your files. The only external resources we use are:

  • CDN Libraries: We load JavaScript libraries (pdf-lib, JSZip) from unpkg CDN for PDF and image processing. These libraries are loaded once and cached by your browser. No user data is sent to these services.
  • No Tracking: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any other tracking services.

5. Cookies

Yourpdfown.com does not use any cookies. The only data we store is your theme preference in localStorage.

6. Data Security

Because your files never leave your device, they benefit from:

  • Your device's built-in security features
  • Your browser's security sandbox
  • No exposure to server-side vulnerabilities
  • No risk of data breaches on external servers

7. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, we cannot distinguish the age of our users. Parents can rest assured that no data from their children is collected or stored.

⚠️ Important Notice

While we provide this privacy-first approach, we cannot guarantee complete security for highly sensitive documents. For extremely confidential documents, we recommend using dedicated desktop software that runs offline.

8. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Because we don't collect personal data, most GDPR rights are inherently satisfied:

  • Right to Access: No personal data is collected, so there's nothing to access.
  • Right to Rectification: No personal data is collected, so there's nothing to correct.
  • Right to Erasure: No personal data is stored (except theme preference which you can clear by clearing your browser's localStorage).
  • Right to Data Portability: Not applicable as no personal data is collected.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect any personal information, changes to this policy will not affect your data in any way.
