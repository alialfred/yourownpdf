How to Use Resize Image on YourOwnPDF.com

Need an image at a specific size? Use YourOwnPDF.com’s free image resizer. All processing runs in your browser — your files never leave your device.

1

Upload Your Image

Click the upload area or drag and drop an image. Supports JPG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF formats up to 85 MB.

  • Drag and drop a file directly onto the upload zone
  • Click the upload area to open file browser
2

Set Dimensions

Choose a quick preset or enter custom width and height. Toggle Maintain aspect ratio to lock proportions.

  • Click any preset button for common sizes
  • Enter exact pixel values in the width/height fields
  • Uncheck aspect ratio to set independent width & height
  • The live preview updates as you adjust
3

Download Your Resized Image

Click Apply Resize to preview the result, then Download to save as PNG.

  • Preview updates instantly with new dimensions
  • Download your resized image as a PNG file
  • Free, private, no sign-up required
