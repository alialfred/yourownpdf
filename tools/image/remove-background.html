How to Remove Background on YourOwnPDF.com

Removing the background from an image doesn’t require expensive software or uploading your files to a remote server. Follow this simple guide to remove backgrounds using YourOwnPDF.com’s free online background remover. All AI processing happens entirely in your browser — your images never leave your device.

1

Upload Your Image

Start by clicking the upload area or dragging an image file from your computer and dropping it into the upload zone. The tool supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP image formats. Once the image is loaded, you will see a preview appear on the right side of the screen, and the file name with its size will be displayed in the upload area. If you selected the wrong file, simply click the Clear button below the upload area to reset and choose a different image. Your original file remains untouched on your device throughout the entire process.

Upload Screen
2

AI Downloads & Processes

After uploading, click the Remove Background button to start. The first time you use this tool, it will download an AI model (~80MB) to your browser cache. This is a one-time download — subsequent uses will be much faster since the model is already cached. A progress bar will appear showing the download and processing status in real time. The AI automatically detects the foreground subject in your image and removes the background with precision. You do not need to select any areas or use lasso tools — everything is handled automatically by the AI model running locally in your browser.

AI Processing
3

Adjust Output Settings

Before downloading, you can customize the output format and background color. For transparent backgrounds, choose PNG format — the removed background area will be fully transparent, making it perfect for logos, product images, and overlays. If you select JPG, you can choose a solid background color using the color picker (white is the default). WEBP offers excellent compression with transparency support, ideal for web use. The preview area will show your image with the background removed, allowing you to verify the result before saving.

Output Settings
4

Download Your Image

Once the AI processing is complete and you have selected your preferred output format, click the green Download button to save your image with the background removed. The file will be automatically named “yourownpdf-no-bg.png” (or the format you selected). The download starts immediately with no additional steps. Your original file is never modified or uploaded to any server — all AI processing stays 100% on your device, ensuring your images remain private and secure. No sign-ups, no uploads, no hidden fees — just fast, free background removal right in your browser.
