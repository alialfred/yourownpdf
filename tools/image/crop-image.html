How to Use Crop Image on YourOwnPDF.com

Need to cut out a specific area of an image? Use YourOwnPDF.com’s free image cropper. All processing runs in your browser — your files never leave your device.

1

Upload Your Image

Click the upload area or drag and drop an image. Supports JPG, PNG, and WEBP formats up to 85 MB.

  • Drag and drop a file directly onto the upload zone
  • Click the upload area to open file browser
2

Set Crop Area

Choose an aspect ratio preset or enter exact X, Y, width, and height values. Use Free for unrestricted cropping.

  • Click any ratio preset for standard aspect ratios
  • Enter pixel values for precise control
  • The preview updates as you adjust
3

Download Your Cropped Image

Click Apply Crop to preview the result, then Download to save as PNG.

  • Preview updates instantly after cropping
  • Download your cropped image as a PNG file
  • Free, private, no sign-up required
