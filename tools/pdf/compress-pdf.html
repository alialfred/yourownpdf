How to Compress PDF on YOUROWNPDF.COM

1

Upload Your PDF

Click on the upload area or drag and drop your PDF file. The file stays on your device — nothing is uploaded to any server.

2

Choose Compression Level

Select "Low" for best quality, "Medium" for a balance, or "High" for the smallest file size. The compression happens locally on your device.

3

Download Result

Once compressed, download your smaller PDF file. It will be saved as "yourownpdf-compressed.pdf" automatically.
