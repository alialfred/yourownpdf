How to Use YourOwnPDF.com

1

Upload Your PDF

Click on the upload area or drag and drop your PDF file. Only PDF (.pdf) files are supported.

  • Drag and drop a file directly onto the upload zone
  • Click the upload area to open file browser
2

Select Rotation Angle

Choose how much you want to rotate your pages: 90°, 180°, or 270°. All pages will be rotated by the same amount.

  • 90° — rotate clockwise a quarter turn
  • 180° — flip upside down
  • 270° — rotate counter-clockwise a quarter turn
3

Click Rotate PDF

Click the "Rotate PDF" button. The rotation happens entirely in your browser — your file never leaves your device.

  • Wait for the rotation to complete (usually seconds)
  • A success message will appear when done
  • The "Download" button will become available
4

Download Your Rotated PDF

Click the green "Download" button to save your rotated PDF file. The file will be named "yourownpdf-rotated.pdf".

  • Your rotated PDF preserves original quality
  • All pages are rotated by the angle you selected
  • Ready to share or print immediately
