How to Use Organize PDF on YourOwnPDF.com

1

Upload Your PDF

Click the upload area or drag and drop any PDF file. All pages are listed after upload.

  • Drag and drop a file directly onto the upload zone
  • Click the upload area to open file browser
2

Rearrange & Delete Pages

Reorder pages by dragging the ☰ handle, or use the ▲/▼ arrow buttons (great for touch devices). Click the × button to remove pages you don’t need.

  • Drag a page up or down to change its position
  • On mobile, tap ▲ or ▼ to move a page
  • Moved pages turn green so you can see your changes
  • Click × to delete a page
3

Save & Download

Click Save & Download to create a new PDF with your changes. All processing is done in your browser on YourOwnPDF.com.

  • Original quality is preserved
  • Your files never leave your device
  • Free, private, no sign-up required
