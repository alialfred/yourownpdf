How to Use Split PDF on YourOwnPDF.com

1

Upload Your PDF

Click the upload area or drag and drop your PDF file. Only one PDF is needed. Your file stays entirely on your device — nothing is uploaded to any server.

  • Drag and drop your PDF onto the upload zone
  • Click the upload area to browse for a file
  • Only PDF files are supported
2

Select Pages to Extract

Use the "From" and "To" fields to specify the page range you want to extract. For example, enter 3 and 8 to extract pages 3 through 8.

  • Set the starting page number in the "From" field
  • Set the ending page number in the "To" field
  • The total page count updates automatically after upload
3

Click Split PDF

Click the green "Split PDF" button. The extraction happens instantly in your browser. A success message will appear when it's complete.

  • Extraction is 100% client-side
  • Original quality is preserved
  • Ready in seconds
4

Download Your Pages

Click the green "Download" button to save your extracted PDF pages as a new file named "yourownpdf-split.pdf".

  • The extracted pages are combined into a single PDF
  • All formatting and quality are preserved
  • Ready to share or print immediately
