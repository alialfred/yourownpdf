How to Use Merge PDF on YourOwnPDF.com

1

Upload Your PDF Files

Click on the upload area or drag and drop your PDF files. You can select multiple files at once using Ctrl/Cmd + click, or select an entire folder. The supported format is PDF (.pdf).

  • Drag and drop files directly onto the upload zone
  • Click the upload area to open file browser
  • Add more files anytime by clicking the upload area again
2

Arrange File Order (Optional)

Before merging, you can reorder your files to ensure they appear in the correct sequence in the final document. The first file in the list will be first in the merged PDF.

  • Click a file to select it (highlighted in blue)
  • Drag the handle (☰) on the left to reorder
  • Drop the file in your desired position
  • Remove unwanted files using the X button
3

Click Merge PDF

Once you've added all your files (minimum 2 required), click the "Merge PDF" button. The merging process happens entirely in your browser — your files never leave your device.

  • Wait for the merging to complete (usually seconds)
  • A success message will appear when done
  • The "Download" button will become available
4

Download Your Merged PDF

Click the green "Download" button to save your merged PDF file. The file will be named "yourownpdf-merged.pdf" and saved to your downloads folder.

  • Your merged PDF preserves original quality
  • All pages are combined in the order you specified
  • Ready to share or print immediately
