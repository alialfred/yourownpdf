How to Use YOUROWNPDF.COM — Merge PDF

1

Add Your PDF Files

Click on the upload area or drag and drop your PDF files. You can select multiple files at once. All files are processed locally on your device — nothing is uploaded to any server.

2

Reorder if Needed

The files will appear in the order they were added. To change the merge order, simply drag and drop the file items in the list. The first file will be first in the final merged PDF.

3

Click Merge

Once you've added all your files and set the order, click the "Merge PDF" button. The merging happens instantly in your browser using our powerful PDF processing engine.

4

Download Your File

When the merge is complete, a download button will appear. Click it to download your merged PDF file. The file will be named "yourownpdf-merged.pdf" by default.
